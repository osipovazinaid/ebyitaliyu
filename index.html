Description

The top soil is a screened local soil that is clay based and is best used as a filler. Because we use this product in our homes, we take extra care to screen the soil to remove any unnecessary debris like stones, yard waste, garbage and anything that can affect an "even surface" of your yard.

This top soil is all about making uneven ground even or sculpting the base of your landscape as you see fit to meet your vision of a beautiful backyard or frontyard. Not only is our topsoil an excellent underlay for fresh sod, it can also be used to plant certain hardy shrubs or trees. For plants with greater nutrient needs, we highly recommend that you use our specially formulated veggie soil for growing grass (seeds), vegetables and/or flowers.

1 Cubic Yard Weight = 2000lbs (approx.)
